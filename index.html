FAQ

+

Rangvistar paints ki cost kya hoti hai?

Paint ki cost surface area, paint type (emulsion, enamel, distemper), aur quantity par depend karti hai. Accurate estimate ke liye humein contact karein, taaki hum aapke requirements samajh kar aapko best price de sakein.

+

Delivery aur paint supply mein kitna samay lagta hai?

Delivery time usually 2-5 business days hota hai, lekin bulk orders ya customized requirements ke liye thoda aur samay lag sakta hai. Order place karte waqt hum aapko exact timeline bata dete hain.

+

Kya aap painting services bhi provide karte hain?

Haan, Rangvistar aapko trusted painting professionals ke through complete painting solution provide karta hai. Aap humein apni site details dein, hum aapko best service aur quotation denge.

+

Kya aap mere ghar ke liye paint suggest kar sakte hain?

Bilkul! Humare color experts aapki wall condition, lighting aur interior design ke hisaab se aapko best colors aur finishes recommend karte hain. Aap humein call ya message karke consultation book kar sakte hain.

+

Payment kab aur kaise karna hota hai?

Payment order confirmation ke samay partial aur delivery ke time final hota hai. Hum online payments, UPI, credit/debit cards, aur bank transfers accept karte hain. Customized payment plans bhi available hain for bulk or regular clients.
